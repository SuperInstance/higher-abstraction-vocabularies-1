# Higher Abstraction Vocabularies (HAV)

**An exhaustive vocabulary engine — 401 terms across 91 domains — for agents and humans doing research.**

> Every term compresses paragraphs of explanation into a single word. With the right vocabulary, one word carries more meaning than a thousand words of description.

## What It Is

HAV is a structured, searchable vocabulary where every term includes:
- **Short definition** (one line)
- **Full description** with fleet integration and real-world examples
- **Cross-domain bridges** connecting concepts across 91 fields
- **Abstraction levels** from Concrete(0) to Meta(4)
- **Antonyms** where applicable
- **Tags** for filtering and discovery

## Design Philosophy

1. **Precision over coverage** — every term is deeply explained, not shallowly listed
2. **Bridges over boundaries** — cross-domain connections reveal shared mathematical structure
3. **Fleet-integrated** — terms reference actual cuda-* crates
4. **Multi-audience** — useful for agents, developers, and researchers
5. **Grounded in practice** — fleet-specific examples, not just academic definitions
6. **Living vocabulary** — growing toward 500+ terms
